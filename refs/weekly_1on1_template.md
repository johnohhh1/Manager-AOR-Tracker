Use these to keep conversations flowing naturally:

**Building Rapport:**
- "What was the best part of your week?"

- "What's motivating you right now?"
- "What would make your shifts even better?"
- "Who on the team are you learning from?"

- "What's been frustrating this week?"
- "If you could change one thing, what would it be?"
- "What obstacles are getting in your way?"

**Career Development:**
- "Where do you see yourself in 6 months?"
- "What skills do you want to develop?"
- "What type of schedule would be ideal for you?"

**Gathering Feedback:**
- "What am I doing well as your manager?"
- "What could I do differently to support you better?"
- "How can our team work better together?"
